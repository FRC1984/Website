Our Team

Welcome to the Jawas FRC 1984. The Jawas are a FIRST Robotics team based out of Overland Park, KS. Formed in 2007, this is the team’s 10th year of competition. Our team works hard to spread the message of FIRST in our school, community, and world.

Robot Camp

The day camp combines LEGO building, computer programming, design, team-work, and problem-solving skills in a fun and challenging environment. The day camp is specifically designed for younger students that are interested in exploring robotics using the Lego NXT systems from Lego Education.
