Join Our Team

If you would like to join our team, please contact us and let us know. New members are always welcome, and everyone is encouraged to join. We meet after school on Tuesdays and Fridays, and everyday during build season. Our subteams consist of building, programming, design, electrical, CAD, and web developement, so there is a place for everyone!
